Parameter index

The GHP-417, representing General Unmanned Helicopter Platform, can fly day-after-day, night-after-night, in adverse weather conditions, for up to 4 hours with max payload 130 kg at a time, on an accurate flight path under computer control.

Application scenarios

The GHP-417 is substantially safer than a full sized helicopter since it can auto rotate to the ground in a controlled landing with a complete failure of the main engine. Although full size helicopters may be able to perform this function with a highly skilled pilot, it often results in significant damage to the aircraft as well as injury to the pilot.

Strategic cooperation

GHP-417 can fly safely at low altitudes to provide a variety of inspection functions such as; pipelines, IEDs, railways, etc. GHP-417 updates a server computer in real time in flight, allowing users to view the latest information, via the Internet from any terminal designated by the operators.
